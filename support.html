Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Before reaching out, please check our FAQ section to see if your question has already been answered.

  • Q1: How do I use SnapReader? A1: Toggle the silent/ring switch on the side of your iPhone, you should not see a color indicator. Swipe down from the top of the screen to disable Focus Mode and enable sound. Check if the audio output is set to headphones or the speaker. To enable app permissions, go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera > SnapReader, then tap the switch on the right to grant.
  • Q2: What should I do if I experience issues? A2: Please follow A1's steps. If still meet an issue, please email me.
